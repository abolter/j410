We're surrounded by bugs everywhere we go. There are over 30,000 species of bugs in California alone. Insects play an important role in our ecosystems from pollination to food sources for other species.

Natalie's Bees

Natalie Tokhmakhian, an electrical engineering graduate student at Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo, describes her first time catching a swarm of bees.She dabbled a little bit in beekeeping with her dad back home, but didn't really get into it until she started taking Beekeeping (PLSC 175). She is enjoying the class so much, she even started her own beeswax chapstick business and will donate the money she makes back into the beekeeping class.
